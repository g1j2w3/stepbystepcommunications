Meet Elverage Allen

Elverage Allen, the driving force behind Step By Step Communications, has revolutionized media advertising with his strategic and innovative approach. Under his leadership, the agency soared, increasing advertising sales revenue from $3.7 million to over $34.5 million in four years. His expertise in elevating new and emerging television networks has markedly increased their visibility and impact in the industry. Recognized for his outstanding contribution to diversity marketing, Allen was named the Ad Sales Executive of the Year by Broadcast & Cable Magazine in 2016. He is renowned for his ability to forge strong partnerships across major advertising markets and for his skill in developing campaigns that resonate with diverse audiences. Allen's leadership continues to steer Step By Step Communications towards new heights of success and influence in the media world.
